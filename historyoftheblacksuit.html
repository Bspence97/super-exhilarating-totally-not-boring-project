THE HISTORY OF THE BLACK SUIT




















The black suit Spiderman wears was originally conceptualized by a fan named Randy Schueller in 1982. In Secret Wars #8, Spidey mistakes a mysterious black substance for Reed Richards's costume creator and it immediately takes hold of him. He did not know it at the time, but this was an alien symbiote. This new suit comes with a host of new abilities including increased strength and the ability for it to produce its own webbing.
